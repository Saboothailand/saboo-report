2. Project Background & Rationale

Project Purpose: Overcoming existing facility limitations and establishing infrastructure for future growth

🏭 Space Constraints

Critical shortage of production and storage space resulting in decreased operational efficiency and logistics bottlenecks

⚙️ Process Inefficiencies

Unsegregated manufacturing areas increasing cross-contamination risks and reducing productivity

🏆 Quality Certification Limitations

'Handmade' grade restrictions limiting external credibility and marketing capabilities

📈 Limited Business Scalability

Insufficient infrastructure for new formulations (liquid) and product categories (pet care)

🏢 Office Space Inefficiency

Poor office layout and underutilization leading to decreased operational efficiency

👥 Organizational Capacity Gaps

Insufficient marketing and operations departments limiting business expansion capabilities

3. Quantitative Results: Production Infrastructure Innovation

Production Capacity Enhancement Overview

📦 Facility Expansion
